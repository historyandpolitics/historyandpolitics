The Watergate Break-In



The Break-In:

In May of 1972, Richard Nixon, who was the President of the United States at that time, sent out five men to break into the Watergate hotel and office complex. Their goal was to bug the offices of the Democratic National Committee, to help Nixon win the 1972 election.

But the wiretaps didn't work properly, so on June 17th, the group returned to the Watergate complex. Though things didn't really go according to plan. The Watergate burglers were arrested at 2:30 AM.
